New system centralizes all curbside transactions, improving visibility and operational efficiency Eureka, Calif. (April 13, 2026) – In partnership with Passport, the City of Eureka, California, is enhancing its parking operations through a unified platform. The newly implemented system gives the city modern tools to strengthen parking compliance and more effectively oversee curbside payments.
